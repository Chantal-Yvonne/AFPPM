About Us

We make project delivery simple, effective, and impactful.

AF Project and Programme Management (Pty) Ltd is a South Africa-based, women-led consulting firm providing end-toend project and programme management solutions to organisationsseeking strategic delivery, operational excellence, and measurable impact. With proven experience managing multi-million-rand transformation projects in both corporate and development sectors, AFPPM brings a unique blend of governance, structure, and social awareness to every engagement.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is the advantage of hiring a consultant instead of doing it in-house?

Hiring a consultant brings specialised expertise, objectivity, and efficiency that internal teams may lack while managing day-to-day operations. At Af Project and Programme Management, we apply proven frameworks and governance models to optimise performance, reduce risks, and deliver measurable outcomes, allowing your team to focus on core business priorities.

What kind of deliverables are to be expected?

Clients can expect clear, structured deliverables including project plans, governance frameworks, stakeholder engagement strategies, monitoring and evaluation reports, and performance dashboards. Each deliverable is designed to enhance transparency, accountability,and long term impact- ensuring your projects stay on time, budget, and aligned with strategic goals.

How long will the project take and how long until results can be measured?

Timelines depend on the project's complexity and scope, but we prioritise efficiency and early wins. AFPPM typically establishes measurable milestones within the first quarter of engagement, with visible performance improvements by the second.
